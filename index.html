Dev Soundboard.

The Dev Soundboard is a collection of sounds perfect for many occasions you might find yourself in as a Dev such as finally getting around to fixing that weird "copy paste" bug you introduced 2 years ago, or something more dismal such as breaking the build. Also, has the added benefit of increasing job security because even you you can't program, with this app, you can still be the "soundFX" guy...
